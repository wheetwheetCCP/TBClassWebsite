About Wandia

The name Wandia is a Kenyan feminine name. In the Kikuyu culture the name Wandia means a: Fierce Warrior, Strong and Idelistic. People with this name desire harmony, are mentally quick and intuitive and they appear shy.
With this in mind i go through every day as a fierce warrior doing differnt, new and interesting things. Here are some of the things i'm into;

Swimming

I started learning how to swim in the 5th grade and i loved ever since. The main reason i love swimming is that it's very relaxing.I love the feeling of floating on water and feeling weightless. I also love how competitive it is as a sport.

Watching movies

I love watching movies because for that one hour or two i can get away from everything and be entertained by someone's creativity. I also love watching movies especially comedy because it is proven that laughter is the best medicine.

Cooking

Food is like art except you get to eat it when your are done.It's very fulfilling to see something you created with your own hands, its better to eat it too... Food creates a sense of community,regardless of how small or big and that's why i love cooking.
