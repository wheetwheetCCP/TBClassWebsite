all about e.reid dreams and nightmares

who am i? I do not remember much of my early childhood, but my mom said that I was a very active, curious, and communicative child. But from a young age, i was taught to hold my own, stand up for myself and ignore all the hate that a lot of people seem to feel toward others. Born and raised in Philadelphia it’s subtly ingrained in you that you are not going to be the type of person who takes shit from anyone.


my ups and downs* I understand that life is not a bed of roses, but you have to stay positive. Nothing in my life was ever given to me i had to earn it. In junior high and high school i had to fight to get to school and fight to get back to my neighborhood. But i learned that education can take you as far as you want it to.


conclusion! No matter how many times a person gets knocked down as long as they continue to get up and move on they are and will always be successful. Have faith in yourself.
